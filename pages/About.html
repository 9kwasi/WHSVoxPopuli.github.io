About us

Vox Populi - The Voice of the People

We are a community outreach/telecommunications based club that provides community members with both a physical and digital medium in which to express their ideas. Vox Populi exists primarily to provide a medium, an audience, and moderation for those who wish to express their ideas through discussion, seminar, and/or lecture. We hope to actively engage others in topics presented intuitively and maintain a high level of discussion for anyone to join.

We are NOT constraining discussions to specifically philosophy, or topics of socio-political nature. Vox Populi hopes to serve as a means to express ideas in general; everything from Religious Tolerance to Global Apathy to Linguistics to the Economics of Illicit Industrial Pharmacology are all welcome topics.

Symposiums

Every couple of weeks we will be assembling symposiums consisting of 3-5 speakers that will be giving 10 minute talks on whatever topic they choose. Speakers may choose to have time allotted after their talk to discuss/debate any ideas presented.

Any member of the community can sign up to speak for a symposium. We have a committee that will vet speakers beforehand to check for preparedness and school appropriateness. This committee is also a resource available to less experienced speakers who are seeking assistance for their upcoming symposium talk.

All talks will be posted online on our website.
